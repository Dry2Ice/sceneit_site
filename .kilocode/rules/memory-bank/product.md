# Product Context: SceneIt

## Why This Exists

SceneIt is a cinema community platform for cinephiles worldwide. The name is a wordplay on "scene" and "seen it" — referencing both film scenes and having watched films. It brings together forum discussions, expert reviews, news coverage, and interactive quizzes into a single destination.

## Problems It Solves

1. **Fragmented community**: Cinema discussions are scattered across multiple platforms
2. **Lack of depth**: Most movie sites offer superficial content — SceneIt aims for expert-level discourse
3. **Engagement**: Quizzes and voting gamify the cinema experience beyond passive reading
4. **Presentation**: Most cinema sites look dated — SceneIt has a AAA-gaming/Hollywood blockbuster aesthetic

## User Flows

### First-time visitor
1. Lands on cinematic hero → sees the SceneIt logo, "Every Frame Tells a Story" tagline
2. Scrolls through three section strips → understands Riot Reel, Flick Feed, Binge Buddy
3. Sees founders → builds trust in the team
4. Registers via the form → joins the community

### Returning user
1. Checks latest news and reviews in Flick Feed
2. Participates in forum discussions and votes in Riot Reel
3. Takes weekly quizzes and competes on leaderboards in Binge Buddy

## Key User Experience Goals

- **AAA presentation quality**: Cinematic, immersive design rivaling game/film marketing sites
- **Smooth animations**: Scroll-triggered reveals, parallax, staggered entrances
- **Dark cinema aesthetic**: Near-black (#07070a) with amber/gold accents
- **Clear section identity**: Each section has unique color scheme and personality
- **Mobile-first**: Fully responsive for phone/tablet browsing
- **Fast**: Server Components for instant page loads

## Three Main Sections

1. **Riot Reel** (violet) — Community debates, polls, director showdowns
2. **Flick Feed** (rose/red) — Expert reviews, breaking news, festival coverage
3. **Binge Buddy** (amber/orange) — Trivia quizzes, frame challenges, leaderboards
